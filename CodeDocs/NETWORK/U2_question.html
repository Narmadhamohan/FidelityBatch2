Correct TLS/SSL Handshake Process

Step 1: Client sends a "ClientHello" message with supported cipher suites.

Step 2: Server responds with a "ServerHello" and sends its certificate.

Step 3: The client verifies the server's certificate by checking the chain of trust (Root CA, Intermediate CA, Final CA).

Step 4: A symmetric key is established using either Diffie-Hellman or RSA encryption, depending on the negotiated cipher suite.
